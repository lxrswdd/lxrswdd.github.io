About Me

Hello! I’m Xiangrui Liu.
I am a researcher working on video generation, video editing, AIGC detection, VLM hallucination.
I am currently a PhD student at Arizona State University supervised by Dr. Yezhou Yang.
I completed my undergraduate studies and Master’s degree at the University of British Columbia where my supervisors were Dr. Julian Cheng and Dr. Shan Du.

I am open to research internship opportunities and academic collaborations. Feel free to reach out to me via email xiangrui@asu.edu

Research Interests:

  • Long Video Editing
  • Video Relighting
  • Video Generation
  • AIGC Detection
  • VLM Hallucination
